Frequently asked

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade any time — billing is pro-rated. Annual switches refund the unused portion.

What happens after the 7-day trial?

If you don't enter payment, the account drops to the Free plan. No charge, no surprise.

Yes — full Thai tax invoice (ใบกำกับภาษี) via FlowAccount, with your company name and tax ID.

Will my data be deleted if I cancel?

No — your data stays for 90 days. Export reports, keyword history, and audits as CSV/PDF/JSON at any time.

Is there an Enterprise plan?

Yes — for > 1,000 keywords, multi-location, dedicated team, or custom integrations. Contact us.
